Free Software, Free Society

Defending computer user freedom since 1985

The Free Software Foundation is a nonprofit with a worldwide mission to promote computer user freedom and to defend the rights of all software users.

Explore Projects

Our Values

Freedom

Users should be free to run, copy, distribute, study, change, and improve software.

Transparency

Software should be open for inspection, modification, and verification by anyone.

Community

Free software benefits when people work together, sharing knowledge and code.

Our Mission

The Free Software Foundation campaigns for computer users' freedom and for the ethical principles of free software. We develop resources that help people understand and exercise their rights to software freedom.
